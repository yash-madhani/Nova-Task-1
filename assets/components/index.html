Moon

The Moon, Earth's only natural satellite, is a captivating celestial body about 238,855 miles away. It orbits our planet, influencing tidal patterns and showcasing distinct phases as it cycles through its 29.5-day revolution. Its surface is marked by vast plains called maria, created by ancient volcanic activity, alongside cratered highlands and rocky terrains.
